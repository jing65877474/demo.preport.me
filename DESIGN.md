# Codex Share Preview · Visual Identity

## Style Prompt
Light travel-editorial web page adapted for an AI tooling deck: pale blue paper background, navy typography, soft card grids, clipped screenshot/image panels, and a safety-orange hand-drawn underline as the only warm accent. Layout borrows Swiss information hierarchy from the guizang PPT skill, but the mood is more approachable and web-like than strict poster design.

## Colors
- Paper: `#f4f8f8`
- Mist blue: `#c8d9df`
- Ink navy: `#072846`
- Helper blue-gray: `#7f9eaa`
- Accent orange: `#ff6b35`

## Typography
- Sans: Inter, Noto Sans SC, Microsoft YaHei UI
- Mono: JetBrains Mono, Cascadia Code

## What NOT To Do
- No purple/blue gradient SaaS background.
- No dark cyberpunk or neon AI robot imagery.
- No heavy shadows, glassmorphism, or rounded marketing cards.
- No dense paragraphs copied straight from the old PPT.
- No fake logos or generated unreadable UI text inside images.
